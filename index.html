Solomon Naumovich Rabinovich
aka Sholem Aleichem

The man whose middle name is tragicomedy

Here's a time line of Sholem Aleichem's life:

  • 1859 - Born in Pereiaslav, Russian Empire
  • 1874 - composed a Jewish version of the novel Robinson Crusoe
  • 1876 - after graduating from school in Pereiaslav, he began to work as a teacher
  • 1883 - married on Olga, against the wishes of her father, whose estate they inherited a few years later
  • 1884 - first child, a daughter named Ernestina (Tissa)
  • 1887 - born of Daughter Lyalya (Lili)
  • 1888 - born of third daughter, Emma
  • 1889 - Olga gave birth to a son. They named him Elimelech, after Olga's father, but at home they called him Misha
  • 1892 - born of daughter Marusia
  • 1901 - final child, a son named Nochum (Numa)
  • 1905 - left Kyiv (which was fictionalized as Yehupetz) and immigrated to New York City, where he arrived in 1906.
  • 1908 - during a reading tour in Russia, Sholem Aleichem collapsed on a train going through Baranowicze. He was diagnosed with a relapse of acute hemorrhagic tuberculosis and spent two months convalescing in the town's hospital
  • 9999 - should be completed
  • 1914 - Sholem Aleichem moved to New York City again with his family
  • 1916 - died at his Bronx apartment
